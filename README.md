## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our App. However, the App may store the following data locally on your device:

- **Vocabulary Words**: Words and phrases you save for learning (bilingual English-Arabic)
- **Highlights**: Text selections you highlight from conversations (both English and Arabic text)
- **Completed Conversations**: Tracking of conversations you've marked as completed
- **User Profile**: Optional display name for personalization

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Our app features bilingual content (English with Arabic translations) to support Arabic-speaking learners.

### Information Collected Automatically
We may collect certain information automatically when you use our App:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, features used, time spent
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, error reports, performance metrics

### Third-Party Services
Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

#### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may collect the following types of information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (for ad targeting)
- **Ad Interaction Data**: Which ads you view, click, or interact with
- **App Usage Data**: How you use the App and interact with ads

For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please visit:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

#### Google Analytics for Firebase (if implemented)
We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This service collects:
- Usage patterns and feature engagement
- Device information and app performance metrics
- Crash reports and error data

## Your Rights and Choices

- **Access**: Request information about your personal data

- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Objection**: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests
